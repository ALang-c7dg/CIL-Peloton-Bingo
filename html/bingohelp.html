Game Guidelines:

  • Complete classes for the shaded squares on the card.
  • 1:1 class to square ratio. (Except where squares require multiple classes.)
  • Note completion date of class on card square.
  • Completing a longer class than listed on the square counts for square completion.
  • Once complete, take a photo of your card and post it in our Facebook group declaring yourself a BINGO winner.
  • And most important -- HAVE FUN!

Card Notation:

Cycle/Run: 20/30 min-Ally/Andy
Complete either a 20 or 30 minute ride with Ally, OR a 20 or 30 minute run with Andy.
Cycle/Run: 20/30 min-Any German Instructor/Selena
Complete either a 20 or 30 minute ride with any one of the German speaking instructors, OR a 20 or 30 minute run with Selena.
Cycle/Run: 45 min- Jess K/Matt -- Christian V"
Jess and Matt do both cycling and running. Complete either a cycling or running class with either one of them. You could also ride with guest instructor Christian V instead.
Cycle/Run: 45 min- Ben/Emma/Cody -- Becs/Matty
Complete a 45 minute cycling class with Ben, Emma, or Cody (not all three of them), OR complete a 45 minute run with either Becs or Matty.
Cycle/Run: Live DJ - any duration / instructor
Live DJ is the notation used when DJ John Michael teams up with an instructor. The class does not need to be completed "live".
Strength: 2x10 min- Andy/Cody/Jenn/Olivia/Matt/Sam
A lot of the cycle instructors have 10 minute strength classes. For these squares, pick two of the listed instructors and complete a ten minute class with both of your selected instructors. Or, complete one 20 minute class with one of the listed instructors.
Outdoor: 20 min-Jess Sims
A lot of the outdoor classes are usually either running, walking, or both. Feel free to walk these no matter which one you select. Enjoy being outside!
Cardio/Cycle/Run: Intervals / HIIT - any duration
Complete either an Intervals or HITT class in any one of the cardio, cycle, or run categories.
Boot Camp: Any Class
These can be either cycling or running boot camp classes. If the cardio category has boot camp, that will work as well.
Meditation: Any Live Class
As opposed to Live DJ classes, these type of entries do need to be taken from the "Live" schedule. Perfect for completing milestones.
Complete One Monthly Challenge
Peloton offers various challenges each month. Either complete one of the standard miles challenges, or a specialty challenge for that particular month.
Special Guest Class
Peloton intoduced the "Special Guest" leaderboard filter in May of 2021. The filter is available when taking classes on-demand. Filter by "All Time" and "Special Guest" to compete against the special guest. It appears that class names have the special guest listed, such as the ride from 7/15/2021 - 30 min Usain Bolt Dancehall Ride with Hannah Frankson.
